Drought Mitigation Strategies

Water Conservation Techniques

Strategy Description Implementation
Rainwater Harvesting Collecting and storing rainwater for later use Install roof catchment systems and storage tanks
Drip Irrigation Efficient water delivery directly to plant roots Small-scale systems for gardens and farms
Greywater Recycling Reusing household water for irrigation Simple filtration systems for homes

Agricultural Adaptation

Strategy Description Benefit
Drought-resistant crops Varieties that require less water Sorghum, millet, cowpeas
Conservation Agriculture Minimum tillage, mulching Reduces soil moisture loss
Crop Rotation Alternating crops to maintain soil health Improves water retention
